Pepe Romero is a classical and flamenco guitarist. He is well known for his superlative abiltiy to manipulate his instrument's by forming beats while performing songs of extreme technical difficulty.

Born March 8, 1944, Pepe Romero had to endure the devastating effects of the Spanish Civil War and Second World War. Pepe Romero is still performing pieces concerning classical pieces and Spanish Flamencoaround the world.

Having been born in Spain, Romero grew encompassed in the art of flamenco and classical guitar. By age 7, Pepe had performed at the Teatro Lope de Vega for thousands of audience members.
